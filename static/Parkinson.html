Parkinson's Disease Diagnosis

Parkinson's Disease is a brain disorder that leads to shaking, stiffness,
and difficult with balance and coordination. Drawing requires stability of hand
movement. If we give a patient a simple task to draw. We can get an intuition that
it could help us diagnos the disease at some level. Here in this project, The test is simple
the person has to draw any of the two things, a spiral or a wave. Our ML model comes into play,
by extracting features from the Drawing.
